About Us

JK Tyres a reputed Recruiting Agency in City (India). We are specialized manpower consultants for overseas jobs. Our agency is managed by CEO Mr. Taufique Ahmed. We have an experience of more than a decade in this specialized field. We provide manpower resources to our clients in Gulf region mainly in Saudi Arabia.

Vision and Mission

We strive in paving the way for every individual's benefit and success. Our Team We have employed a team of experienced and skilled HR Professionals who are well-versed in their profession and dedicated to provide the most competent HR solutions to the clients. The team endeavors to contact the clients and associates in person.

Why Us? We Aim To:-

Provide the best lot of candidates for jobs in overseas market Enable our associates get a competitive edge by recruiting high potential manpower Develop and maintain professional relationships Assist individuals in realizing their true potential by identifying it for them

Our Strengths

With the help of our dedicated professionals, we are able to provide the most competent manpower solutions to the clients. Further, this helps us in meeting their HR requirements in the efficient manner and enables us to grow strong and lasting relationships. Owing to the above factors, we have carved a name of repute in rendering effective HR consultancy.
